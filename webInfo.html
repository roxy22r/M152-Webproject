Audio

MP3

This audio file is one of the best known. This format saves a lot of storage space. The bit rate is 192 kBit/s and corresponds to CD quality. A big disadvantage of MP3 is the loss of quality in files with low bit rates.

AAC

AAC audio files use less memory than MP3 audio files. Furthermore, the sound quality is the best with AAC but it is sadly not yet supported by all programmes.

WAV

WAV files take up a lot of memory, but it is a good alternative to other audio formats. On the other hand, it is very good for audio editing purposes. In most cases a WAV file is 80% larger than an than an MP3 audio file.
